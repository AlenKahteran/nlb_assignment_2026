Sustainability Reports

Providing Trustworthy and Timely Sustainability Information

Sustainability information is increasingly integrated into investors’ and other stakeholders’ decision making, along with traditional financial data. To this end, NLB Group is committed to providing our stakeholders with comprehensive, relevant, trustworthy, and timely information on sustainability and ESG matters. The reporting is carried out in line with regulatory requirements and reporting frameworks, standards, and recommendations relevant to financial institutions. From the 2024 reporting year onwards, the sustainability report is integrated into the NLB Group Annual Report.
