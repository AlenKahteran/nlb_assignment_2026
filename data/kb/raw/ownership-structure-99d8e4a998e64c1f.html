Ownership structure

The ownership structure of Petrol d.d., Ljubljana (top 10 shareholders) in the first three months of 2026 is almost the same compared to the end of 2025. J&T banka A. S. – Fiduciarni račun is the largest single shareholder, with 5,333,200 shares. It is followed by the Slovenian Sovereign Holding (SDH, d.d.) with 5,299,220 shares and Republic of Slovenia with 4,514,105 shares. Other major shareholders are Kapitalska družba d.d., OTP Banka d.d. – Fiduciarni račun, Erste Group Bank AG – Fiduciarni račun, Vizija Holding, d.o.o., Vizija Holding Ena, d.o.o., Mustand Energy Limited and Perspektiva FT d.o.o.

At the end of March 2026, there were 12.414.479 shares held by foreign legal entities and private individuals, which represents 29.75% of the total. There were 21,929 different shareholders at the end of 2025, but this number increased to 22.208 at the end of March 2026.

Petrol d.d., Ljubljana did not repurchase any treasury shares in the first three months of 2026. Petrol held 614,460 shares in treasury (inclusive 120,400 Petrols’ shares of Geoplin d.o.o. Ljubljana) at the end of March 2026, equivalent to a 1.5% interest in the registered share capital. The Management Board of Petrol d.d., Ljubljana does not have a new authorisation from the General Meeting to purchase treasury shares.

On 1 November 2022, Petrol d.d., Ljubljana, executed the PETG share split in the 1:20 ratio in accordance with the resolution adopted at the 34th General Meeting of Shareholders, after the effective date of the resolution on the amendment of the Articles of Association by entering such amendment into the Register of Companies, executing a corporate action and implementing the stipulated procedures in the Central Securities Register at KDD d.o.o. and Ljubljanska borza, d.d., Ljubljana. The number of PETG shares has increased from 2,086,301 to 41,726,020. The share capital of Petrol d.d., Ljubljana in the amount of EUR 52,240,977.04 has stayed the same.
