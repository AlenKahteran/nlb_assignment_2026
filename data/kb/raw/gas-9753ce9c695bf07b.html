Data-driven gas trading, optimised across Europe

Our gas trading covers a diverse portfolio of standard and structured, physical and financial products within all fields. By combining proprietary forecasts, market insight, strong infrastructure and storage, and close trader-analyst collaboration, we deliver tailored, analytics-backed solutions.

Trusted energy partner active in 13 worldwide markets

We trade across major gas hubs, including TTF, THE, CEGH, PSV, HU VTP, BRM, BHG and Henex, as well as balancing markets in Slovenia and Croatia. We provide access to EU-based storage facilities, ensuring greater flexibility and security of supply.

We also engage in financial gas trading in the North American market, providing broader market insights.
